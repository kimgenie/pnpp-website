SME HR Coaching Program

Most SMEs lack a systematized approach to HR. Without clear criteria, the CEO faces an immense management burden, and employees suffer from low predictability. PNP Partners offers a hands-on coaching approach where system design and execution occur simultaneously.

Service Structure and Timeline

Structured as an initial 6-month contract. Each visit includes a 2-hour deep-dive meeting with management and the HR team.

  • Phase 1: Diagnosis (Month 1): 4 visits/month. Accurate assessment via employee interviews and structured surveys.
  • Phase 2: Design and Execution (Months 2-5): 2 visits/month. Identifying early win tasks, team building, and custom system design.
  • Phase 3: Wrap-up (Month 6): 2 visits/month. Finalizing systems, addressing execution issues, and determining follow-up strategies.
